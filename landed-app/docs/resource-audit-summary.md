# Week 4 Resource Audit Summary

## Key Findings

- Important first-month information is spread across JCU pages, government portals and service-provider websites.
- Some tasks require sequencing because later steps depend on earlier steps.
- High-risk topics must link directly to official sources.
- Every link should display a last-verified date.

## Highest-Priority Tasks

### Before Arrival
- Student’s Pass / IPA documents
- Accommodation preparation

### Arrival Day
- Transport
- Local SIM card

### First Week
- Banking preparation
- Singpass
- LearnJCU
- Attendance requirement

### First Month
- Healthcare
- Campus support services

## Risks

- Official links may become outdated.
- Requirements may change between intakes.
- Financial, medical and visa wording must remain cautious.
- Commercial recommendations must not appear biased.
